Based on the research paper and domain expert feedback, the following refinements have been made to the JSON schema for the Atomic Layer Deposition (ALD) process:

1. **Incorporated Spatial ALD (SALD) Parameters**: The research paper highlights the importance of precursor exposure, substrate velocity, and deposition gap in SALD. These parameters have been added to the schema under a new section `saldParameters`.

2. **Updated Growth Rate Section**: The paper discusses the impact of growth rate on film properties. The `growthRate` section has been updated to include `substrateVelocity` and `precursorExposure`.

3. **Added Film Density**: The paper emphasizes the relationship between growth rate and film density. A `density` property has been added under `filmProperties`.

4. **Revised Descriptions and Units**: Descriptions and units have been revised for clarity and accuracy based on the paper's findings.

5. **Expert Feedback**: The feedback suggested ensuring the schema captures the nuances of different ALD techniques, such as SALD. This has been addressed by adding specific parameters related to SALD.

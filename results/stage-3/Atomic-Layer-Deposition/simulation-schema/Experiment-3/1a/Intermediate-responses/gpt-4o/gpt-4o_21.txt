2. **Updated Reaction Pathways**: The schema now includes detailed pathways for ligand-exchange reactions, incomplete elimination reactions, and nongrowth ligand-exchange reactions. These are crucial for understanding the kinetics and thermodynamics of the ALD process.

3. **Added New Properties for Surface Reactions**: Properties such as `adsorbedComplexStability`, `activationBarrier`, and `reactionFreeEnergy` have been added to capture the energetics of surface reactions, as discussed in the paper.

4. **Enhanced Descriptions and Constraints**: Descriptions for existing properties have been refined to better align with the detailed mechanisms and findings from the research paper. Constraints have been added where necessary to ensure data accuracy.

1. **Incorporation of Plasma-Enhanced ALD (PEALD) Parameters**: The research paper highlights the use of plasma reactants, specifically N2O, in the ALD process. This necessitates the addition of properties related to plasma conditions.

2. **Addition of Doping Information**: The paper discusses nitrogen doping, which is crucial for the ALD process described. A new property for doping has been added to capture this aspect.

3. **Reorganization of Simulation Parameters**: Based on expert feedback, functionals and basis sets have been grouped under methods for better organization.

4. **Clarification of Units**: Ensured that units are consistently used and described, especially for rates, as per expert feedback.

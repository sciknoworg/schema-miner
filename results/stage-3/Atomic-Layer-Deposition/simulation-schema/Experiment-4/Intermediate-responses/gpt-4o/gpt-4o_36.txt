Based on the research paper and domain expert feedback, the following updates and refinements are made to the JSON schema for the Atomic Layer Deposition (ALD) process:

1. **Growth-Per-Cycle (GPC) Correlation**: The research paper highlights the correlation between the growth-per-cycle (GPC) and the surface hydroxyl group concentration. This necessitates the inclusion of a property to capture this correlation.

2. **Surface Hydroxyl Group Concentration**: The paper emphasizes the importance of surface hydroxyl group concentration in the ALD process, particularly in the context of the trimethylaluminum/water process. This property should be explicitly included in the schema.

3. **Reaction Mechanisms**: The paper discusses various reaction mechanisms, including ligand exchange, dissociation, and association. These mechanisms should be clearly defined in the schema.

4. **Steric Hindrance**: The role of steric hindrance in terminating reactions is highlighted. This should be included as a factor in the reaction mechanisms.

5. **Temperature Effects**: The paper discusses the effect of temperature on the reaction mechanisms and GPC. This should be captured in the schema.

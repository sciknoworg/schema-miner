Based on the research paper and domain expert feedback, the following refinements have been made to the JSON schema for the Atomic Layer Deposition (ALD) process:

1. **Incorporation of Hydroxylation and Surface Models**: The paper emphasizes the importance of hydroxylation and different surface models (hydroxylated vs. gibbsite-like). This necessitates the inclusion of properties related to surface models and hydroxylation in the schema.

2. **Reaction Mechanisms and Energetics**: The paper discusses specific reaction mechanisms and their energetics, such as ligand-exchange reactions and the formation of dimethylaluminium (DMA) and monomethylaluminium (MMA). These details should be captured in the schema.

3. **Cooperative Adsorption and Methyl Bridges**: The cooperative effect of adsorbants and the formation of methyl bridges are significant findings that should be reflected in the schema.

4. **Temperature and Pressure Effects**: The paper highlights the impact of temperature and pressure on reaction energetics, which should be considered in the schema.

5. **Inclusion of Specific Reaction Pathways**: The schema should include specific reaction pathways and their associated barriers and energies.

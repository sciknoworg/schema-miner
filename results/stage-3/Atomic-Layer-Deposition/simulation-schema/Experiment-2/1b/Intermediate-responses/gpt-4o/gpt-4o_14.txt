1. **New Properties Added**:
   - `furtherDetails` under `simulationParameters` for additional simulation method details.

2. **Adjustments**:
   - Changed units for `desorptionRate`, `diffusionRate`, and `reactionRate` to "molecules/s" for consistency.
   - Added `plasmaFunctionalization`, `thermodynamicAnalysis`, `dataOrigin`, `groupContributionMethod`, and `computerAidedMolecularDesign` sections to cover additional aspects of the ALD process.

3. **Descriptions and Units**:
   - Improved descriptions and ensured units are consistent and appropriate for each property.

This refined schema now better captures the complexity and nuances of the ALD process, incorporating insights from the research paper and expert feedback.

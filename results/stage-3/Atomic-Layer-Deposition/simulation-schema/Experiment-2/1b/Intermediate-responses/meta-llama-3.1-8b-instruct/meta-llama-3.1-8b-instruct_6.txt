The changes made to the schema include:

1. Merging specific properties like oxygen bridges into a more general property.
2. Grouping binding coefficients under observables, as they are not a separate category.
3. Adding missing properties like input source and methodology details.
4. Simplifying the descriptions of specific groups like DMAI and zirconium precursor.
5. Removing specific groups like adjacent hydrogen-bonded hydroxyl groups and oxygen bridges, as they are not useful.
6. Renaming some properties to make them more descriptive and consistent.
7. Adding unit descriptions to some properties to make them more clear.
8. Removing unnecessary properties and groups to make the schema more concise and easier to understand.

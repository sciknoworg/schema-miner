Based on the research paper and domain expert feedback, the following updates and refinements have been made to the JSON schema for the Atomic Layer Deposition (ALD) process:

1. **Incorporation of Super-Cycle Details**: The research paper introduces the concept of super-cycles for the deposition of LiCoO2, which involves alternating cycles of Co3O4 and Li2CO3. This necessitates the addition of a `superCycleDetails` property under `processConditions` to capture the specifics of these super-cycles.

2. **Addition of Electrochemical Characterization**: The paper discusses the electrochemical characterization of the deposited films, which is crucial for battery applications. A new section `electrochemicalCharacterization` has been added to capture relevant properties such as capacity and charge/discharge behavior.

3. **Inclusion of Elastic Backscattering Spectroscopy (EBS)**: The use of EBS for analyzing film composition is highlighted. This has been added under `characterizationTechniques`.

4. **Refinement of Film Composition Details**: The paper provides detailed insights into the composition of LiCoO2 films, including the Co/Li ratio. This has been incorporated into the `filmProperties` section.

5. **Clarification of Plasma Conditions**: The paper specifies the use of O2 plasma in the deposition process. This has been clarified under `plasmaConditions`.

6. **Addition of Annealing Effects**: The paper discusses the effects of annealing on the film properties, which has been added to the `annealingConditions` under `filmProperties`.

Based on the research paper and domain expert feedback, the following updates and refinements are made to the JSON schema for the ALD process:

1. **New Properties Added**:
   - **plasmaParameters**: Added to capture the influence of plasma parameters on the stoichiometry of the MoOx films.
   - **plasmaStepDuration**: Added to capture the duration of the plasma step, which affects the stoichiometry and performance of the MoOx layers.
   - **plasmaPower**: Added to capture the power of the plasma used, which is crucial for the PE-ALD process.
   - **plasmaSourceType**: Added to specify the type of plasma source used, such as inductively-coupled plasma.
   - **basePressure**: Added to capture the base pressure before starting the deposition, as it is a critical parameter in the process.
   - **reactorPressure**: Added to capture the pressure during the deposition process, which is different from the base pressure.
   - **substratePreparation**: Added to capture the preparation steps of the substrate, such as cleaning and pre-treatment.
   - **opticalProperties**: Updated to include sub-band gap absorption, which is relevant for the MoOx layers.

2. **Existing Properties Modified**:
   - **reactants**: Updated to include specific details about the Mo precursor and its handling, such as heating and transport by carrier gas.
   - **filmProperties**: Updated to include specific details about the MoOx layers, such as stoichiometry and optical properties.
   - **processProperties**: Updated to include specific details about the PE-ALD process, such as plasma parameters and reactor conditions.

3. **Expert Recommendations Applied**:
   - Grouped related properties under new categories such as `plasmaParameters` and `substratePreparation` for better organization.
   - Ensured that the schema remains general and applicable across various ALD processes, not just specific to MoOx.

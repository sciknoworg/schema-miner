### Key Changes:
1. **Title Update**: The title has been updated to better reflect the scope of the schema.
2. **Property Grouping**: Properties have been reorganized based on expert feedback, such as grouping `annealingTemperature` and `annealingAtmosphere` under `annealingConditions`.
3. **New Properties**: Added `dosingTimes` and `purgingTimes` for both precursor and co-reactant.
4. **Removed Properties**: Removed properties like `reactivity`, `counterReactant`, `depositionRate`, `surfaceIntermediates`, `ligandRelease`, `adsorptionSites`, and `effectivePermittivity` as per expert feedback.
5. **Reorganized Properties**: Moved `bandgap` to `electricalProperties` and `dielectricConstant` to `electricalProperties`.
6. **Description Adjustments**: Updated descriptions for clarity and accuracy, such as specifying conditions for `vaporPressure` of co-reactants.

This refined schema aims to provide a comprehensive and accurate representation of the ALD process, incorporating insights from the research paper and expert feedback.

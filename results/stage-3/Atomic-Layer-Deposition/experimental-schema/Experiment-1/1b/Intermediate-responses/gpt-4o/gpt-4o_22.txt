   - Moved `reactor` and `substrate` into `processParameters`.
   - Renamed `processconditions` to `processParameters`.
   - Renamed `cycledetails` to `thicknessControl`.

2. **New Properties and Adjustments:**
   - Added `waterContactAngle` under `filmProperties` to capture hydrophobicity as mentioned in the research paper.

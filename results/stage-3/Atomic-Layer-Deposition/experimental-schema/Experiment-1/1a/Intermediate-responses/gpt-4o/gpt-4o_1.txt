### Key Changes:
1. **Property Merging and Grouping**:
   - Merged `reactor` details into `reactorDesign`.
   - Merged `bubblerTemperatures` into `precursor` under `bubblerTemperature`.
   - Merged `substrate` and `substrateType` under `materialDeposited`.
   - Grouped `bandgap` and `dielectricConstant` under `electrical` properties.
   - Moved `stepCoverage` closer to `conformality`.

2. **Description Adjustments**:
   - Clarified descriptions for `temperatureRange`, `growthPerCycle`, `reactorDesign`, and `density`.
   - Removed `nonHalogenated` property as per feedback.
   - Adjusted `vaporPressure` description for `coReactant` to specify its relevance to thermal ALD.

3. **Structural Organization**:
   - Placed `ALDMethod` and `materialDeposited` at the top of the schema for better organization.

This refined schema aims to be more comprehensive and aligned with the latest research and expert feedback, ensuring it accurately represents the ALD process.

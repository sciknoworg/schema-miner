Based on the research paper and domain expert feedback, the following updates and refinements have been made to the JSON schema for the Atomic Layer Deposition (ALD) process:

1. **New Properties Added**:
   - **Diffusion Barrier Properties**: Added a new section to capture properties related to diffusion barriers, such as failure temperature and specific applications (e.g., Cu and Li diffusion barriers).
   - **Reactor Configuration**: Added details about reactor configuration, including plasma power and gas flow rates, which are critical for understanding the deposition process.

2. **Existing Properties Validated and Modified**:
   - **Material Properties**: Updated the description of material properties to include specific phases and compositions, such as cubic phase for TiN and TaN.
   - **Plasma Parameters**: Enhanced the plasma parameters section to include specific gas mixtures and their flow rates, as these are crucial for the deposition process.

3. **Expert Recommendations Applied**:
   - **Grouping Related Properties**: Grouped related properties under new categories like "Diffusion Barrier Properties" to improve schema organization.
   - **Clarifying Descriptions**: Improved descriptions for existing properties to ensure clarity and alignment with ALD standards.

4. **Avoiding Redundancy**: Ensured that new properties do not overlap with existing ones and maintained the schema's generality.

   - **Plasma Details:** The use of O2 plasma is highlighted, including specific conditions like plasma power and reactor pressure. These details are essential for understanding the plasma-enhanced ALD process.

2. **Refinement of Existing Properties:**
   - **Temperature Range:** The paper discusses a broader temperature range (150°C to 400°C) than previously noted. This range should be reflected in the schema to accommodate the full scope of the process.
   - **Film Properties:** The paper discusses the transition from amorphous to polycrystalline structures at different temperatures, which affects the film's properties. This should be detailed in the schema under film properties.

3. **Clarification and Enhancement:**
   - **GPC (Growth Per Cycle):** The paper provides specific GPC values which indicate the thickness added per ALD cycle. This should be clearly defined in the schema.
   - **Conformality:** The paper discusses the conformality of the HfO2 films on high-aspect-ratio structures. This property should be included to describe the film's ability to coat non-flat surfaces uniformly.

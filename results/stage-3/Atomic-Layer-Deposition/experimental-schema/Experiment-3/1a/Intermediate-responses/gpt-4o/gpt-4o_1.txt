### Key Changes:
1. **Property Grouping and Renaming**: 
   - Grouped `growthPerCycle`, `nucleationPeriod`, and `selfLimitingGrowth` under `growthBehavior`.
   - Moved `opticalBandgap` to `electricalProperties` and renamed it to `bandgap`.
   - Moved `dielectricConstant` to `electricalProperties`.
   - Renamed `pulseTimes` to `dosingTimes` and `purgeTimes` to include specific names for precursor and co-reactant purge times.

2. **Description Updates**:
   - Updated descriptions for `reactorDesign`, `temperatureRange`, and `chamberPressure`.
   - Added units for `carrierDensity` and `mobility`.

3. **Characterization Techniques**:
   - Expanded the list of characterization techniques to include XRD, SEM, XRF, OES, and Spectroscopic Ellipsometry.

4. **Removed Properties**:
   - Removed `bufferLayer`, `surfaceIntermediates`, and `adsorptionSites` as per expert feedback.

This refined schema aims to provide a comprehensive and accurate representation of the ALD process, incorporating insights from the research paper and expert feedback.

Based on the research paper and domain expert feedback, the following updates and refinements have been made to the JSON schema for the Atomic Layer Deposition (ALD) process:

1. **New Properties Added**:
   - **DiffusionBarrierProperties**: Added to capture specific properties related to diffusion barriers, such as failure temperature and barrier material.
   - **ReactorDetails**: Added to capture specific details about the reactor used, including plasma power and gas mixture.

2. **Existing Properties Validated and Modified**:
   - **reactants**: Updated to include specific plasma gas mixtures and exposure times, as these are critical for processes like TiN and TaN deposition.
   - **processConditions**: Enhanced to include more detailed reactor configurations and conditions, such as plasma power and gas flow rates.
   - **filmProperties**: Updated to include specific resistivity values and crystal phases for materials like TiN and TaN.

3. **Expert Recommendations Applied**:
   - Grouped related properties under new categories like `DiffusionBarrierProperties` to better organize the schema.
   - Ensured that the schema captures the variability in material properties based on deposition conditions, as highlighted in the research paper.

4. **Avoided Redundancy and Overspecialization**:
   - Ensured that new properties added are general enough to apply to various ALD processes, not just specific to TiN and TaN.

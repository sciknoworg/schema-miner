Based on the research paper and domain expert feedback, the following updates and refinements have been made to the JSON schema for the Atomic Layer Deposition (ALD) process:

1. **New Properties Added**:
   - **Heterostructures**: Added a new section to capture details about heterostructures, which are crucial for applications like photoelectrochemical water splitting.
   - **Post-Deposition Treatment**: Added properties to capture post-deposition treatments such as sulfurization, which are important for improving film crystallinity and performance.

2. **Existing Properties Validated and Modified**:
   - **Growth Behavior**: Updated the description to include the importance of self-limiting characteristics and growth per cycle, as highlighted in the paper.
   - **Film Properties**: Enhanced the description of crystallinity and added details about amorphous vs. crystalline states, as discussed in the paper.
   - **Reactants**: Added details about specific precursors like C7H8Mo(CO)3 and their role in the ALD process.

3. **Expert Recommendations Applied**:
   - **Grouping Related Properties**: Grouped related properties under new sections like "Heterostructures" and "Post-Deposition Treatment" for better organization.
   - **Avoiding Redundancy**: Ensured that new properties do not overlap with existing ones and maintain the schema's generality.

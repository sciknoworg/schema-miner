Based on the research paper and domain expert feedback, the following updates and refinements have been made to the JSON schema for the Atomic Layer Deposition (ALD) process:

1. **New Properties Added**:
   - **Water Contact Angle**: Added to capture the hydrophobicity of the film, which is a significant property for Y2O3 films as discussed in the paper.
   - **Surface Free Energy**: Added to provide a measure of the film's wettability, which is inversely related to the water contact angle.
   - **Annealing Effects on Wettability**: Added to capture changes in water contact angle post-annealing, as the paper discusses the stability of hydrophobicity after annealing.

2. **Refinements**:
   - **Precursor Details**: Updated to include specific details about the liquid precursor Y(EtCp)2(iPr-amd) used for Y2O3 deposition.
   - **Growth Per Cycle**: Adjusted to reflect the specific growth rates for different processes (thermal and plasma-enhanced) as mentioned in the paper.
   - **Film Density**: Added specific density values for Y2O3 films deposited using different reactants, as these are critical for understanding film quality.

3. **Expert Feedback Application**:
   - **Grouping Related Properties**: Grouped properties related to film wettability and annealing effects under a new section for clarity.
   - **Avoiding Redundancy**: Ensured that new properties do not overlap with existing ones and are specific to the findings of the research paper.

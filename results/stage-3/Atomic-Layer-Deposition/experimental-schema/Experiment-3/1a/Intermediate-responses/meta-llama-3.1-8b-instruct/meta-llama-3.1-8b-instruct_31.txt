The following new properties were added:

* `DepositionTemperature`: The deposition temperature in °C.
* `PrecursorReactivity`: The reactivity of the precursor.
* `CoReactantReactivity`: The reactivity of the co-reactant.
* `CarrierGasFlowRate`: The flow rate of the carrier gas in sccm.
* `PlasmaPowerDensity`: The power density of the plasma in W/cm^2.
* `SurfaceRoughness`: The surface roughness in nm.
* `FilmThickness`: The film thickness in nm.
* `GrowthRate`: The growth rate in Å/cycle.
* `CrystalOrientation`: The crystal orientation of the film.
* `ImpurityContent`: The impurity content of the film, including hydrogen, carbon, nitrogen, oxygen, and fluorine.

The following properties were modified:

* `ProcessParameters`: The `PrecursorDosingTime`, `CoReactantDosingTime`, `PrecursorPurgingTime`, and `CoReactantPurgingTime` properties were added to the `ProcessParameters` object.
* `SurfacePassivation`: The `PlasmaExposureTime` property was added to the `SurfacePassivation` object.
* `MaterialProperties`: The `ChemicalComposition` property was modified to include the `Elements` and `Stoichiometry` properties.
* `OtherAspects`: The `Safety`, `FilmStability`, `Reproducibility`, `LiteratureComparison`, `DevicePerformance`, and `PlasmaInducedDamage` properties were added to the `OtherAspects` object.
* `NitrideInterlayer`: The `Thickness` and `Composition` properties were added to the `NitrideInterlayer` object.
* `PlasmaComposition`: The `H2Fraction` and `NH3Production` properties were added to the `PlasmaComposition` object.
* `ReactionProducts`: The `HCp`, `C2H3`, `C3H3`, `PlasmaRadicals`, and `PlasmaSurfaceInteraction` properties were added to the `ReactionProducts` object.

The following properties were removed:

* `AnnealingProcess`: This property was removed as it is not relevant to the ALD process.

### Key Updates:

2. **Plasma Properties**: Introduced `plasmaProperties` under `coReactant` to capture plasma power and exposure time, reflecting the importance of plasma conditions in the deposition process.
3. **Growth Behavior**: Grouped related properties under `growthBehavior` as suggested by the domain expert.

5. **Conformality**: Emphasized the importance of conformality in `structuralProperties` based on the research paper's focus on conformal coatings for solid-state batteries.

Note that I have added a new property called `diketoneEtchingMechanism` to the schema, which includes sub-properties for `hfacAdsorption`, `hfacSurfaceSpecies`, and `surfaceCleaning`. These sub-properties are used to describe the diketone etching mechanism, including the adsorption energy and configuration of the hfac molecule, the surface coverage and configuration of the hfac surface species, and the surface cleaning method and efficiency. I have also added new definitions for `materialPropertyUniformity` and `materialSelectivity` to the schema, which are used to describe the material property uniformity and selectivity of the etch process, respectively.

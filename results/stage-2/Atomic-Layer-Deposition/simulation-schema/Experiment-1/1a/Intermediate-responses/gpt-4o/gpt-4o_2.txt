2. **Addition of Energetics and Reaction Pathways**: The paper discusses the energetics of reactions and the formation of stable intermediate complexes. Properties related to reaction energetics and pathways have been added.

3. **Clarification of Material Properties**: The paper emphasizes the importance of the starting surface and the presence of SiO2 layers. This has been reflected in the materials section.

4. **Expert Feedback**: The feedback suggests ensuring that the schema captures the complexity of surface interactions and the role of intermediate complexes.

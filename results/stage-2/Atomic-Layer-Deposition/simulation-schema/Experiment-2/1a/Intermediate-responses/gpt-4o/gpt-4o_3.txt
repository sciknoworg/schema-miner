   - **Reaction Mechanisms**: Updated to include specific pathways and reactions observed in the study, such as ligand-exchange reactions and combustion reactions.

3. **Expert Recommendations Applied**:
   - **Grouping Related Properties**: Grouped properties related to precursor chemistry and co-reactant interactions for better organization.

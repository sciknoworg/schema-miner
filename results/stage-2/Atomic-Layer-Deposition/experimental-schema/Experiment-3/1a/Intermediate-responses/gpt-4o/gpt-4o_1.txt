### Key Changes:
1. **New Properties Added**: 
   - `ALDMethod`, `materialDeposited`, `carrierGas`, `bubblerTemperature`, `reactor`, `nucleationPeriod`, `selfLimitingGrowth`, `substrate`, `crystallinity`, `roughness`, and `density`.

2. **Property Adjustments**:
   - `growthPerCycle` now uses "nm/cycle" as the unit.
   - Removed default temperature range.
   - `reactivity` was removed from reactants and should be considered under process conditions or film properties.

3. **Descriptions Enhanced**:
   - Improved descriptions for clarity and specificity, especially for properties like `filmStability` and `reproducibility`.

This refined schema now better captures the complexity and specific requirements of the ALD process, incorporating insights from the research paper and expert feedback.

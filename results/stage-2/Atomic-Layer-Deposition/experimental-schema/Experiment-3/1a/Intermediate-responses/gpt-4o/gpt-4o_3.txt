   - **VaporPressure**: Added under `reactants.precursor` to capture the vapor pressure of the precursor, which is crucial for understanding its volatility and reactivity.
   - **ReactorDesign**: Added under `processConditions` to capture specific design features of the reactor that may influence deposition, such as the presence of a static mixer.

2. **Modified Properties**:
   - **TemperatureRange**: Updated to include a more detailed description, emphasizing the importance of low-temperature deposition for certain precursors.
   - **Conformality**: Enhanced description to reflect the 100% step coverage achieved in high aspect ratio structures.

3. **Expert Recommendations Applied**:
   - **Reactor**: Added a nested property `reactorDesign` to capture specific design features that influence deposition.
   - **FilmProperties**: Grouped related properties under `opticalProperties` and `electricalProperties` to better organize the schema.

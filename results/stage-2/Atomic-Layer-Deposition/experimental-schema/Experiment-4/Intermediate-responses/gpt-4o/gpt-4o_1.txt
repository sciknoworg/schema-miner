Based on the research paper and domain expert feedback, the following refinements have been made to the JSON schema for the Atomic Layer Deposition (ALD) process:

1. **New Properties Added**:
   - **Film Density**: Added to capture the density of the deposited film, which varies with temperature.
   - **Hydrogen Concentration**: Added to account for the hydrogen content in the film, which increases at lower temperatures.
   - **Refractive Index**: Added under `opticalProperties` to capture the refractive index variation with temperature.
   - **Gas Permeability**: Added under `filmProperties` to capture the gas diffusion barrier properties, especially relevant for polymer substrates.

2. **Modified Properties**:
   - **Temperature Range**: Updated to allow for lower temperature values, reflecting the low-temperature ALD processes.
   - **Cycle Details**: Added a property to capture the cycle time details, which vary significantly with temperature.

3. **Expert Feedback Applied**:
   - Grouped related properties under `filmProperties` and `processConditions` for better organization.
   - Ensured that all properties have clear descriptions and appropriate data types.

### Key Changes:
1. **ALD Method and Material Deposited**: Added properties to specify the ALD method and the material being deposited.
2. **Reactants**: Expanded to include detailed descriptions and bubbler temperatures for both precursors and co-reactants, as well as plasma properties for PEALD.
3. **Process Properties**: Added properties for reactor type, nucleation period, self-limiting growth, and substrate type. Adjusted units for pressure to bar.
4. **Film Properties**: Added structural properties such as crystallinity, roughness, and density. Grouped related properties under structural, electrical, and optical categories.
5. **Safety and Stability**: No changes were made, as the existing properties were deemed sufficient.

These changes ensure the schema is comprehensive and accurately reflects the ALD process, particularly for low-temperature applications as discussed in the research paper.
